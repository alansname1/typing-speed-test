Typing Practice for Children

This page helps children practice typing simple sentences to improve typing speed and accuracy.

Easy Typing Practice Sentences

Type these sentences slowly and try to avoid mistakes.

  • The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
  • I like to practice typing every day.
  • Learning to type helps with school work.
  • Practice makes typing faster and easier.
  • Computers are fun when you can type well.
