Top Typing Speeds Today

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). Many people improve their typing speed through regular practice and keyboard familiarity.

Examples of Fast Typing Speeds

  • 40 WPM – Average typing speed
  • 60 WPM – Above average typing speed
  • 80 WPM – Advanced typing speed
  • 100 WPM – Very fast typist
  • 120+ WPM – Professional typing speed

World Record Typing Speeds

Some of the fastest typists in the world have reached speeds above 200 words per minute using specialized training and extensive practice.

Typing speed competitions and online typing tests allow people to challenge themselves and improve their keyboard skills.

How to Improve Typing Speed

Improving typing speed takes practice and patience. Here are some tips:

  • Practice typing daily for 10 minutes
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Learn proper finger placement
  • Use online typing tests regularly
