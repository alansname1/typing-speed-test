Average Typing Speed by Age

Typing speed is usually measured in words per minute, also called WPM. People of different ages tend to type at different speeds depending on experience, practice, and how often they use a computer.

Average Typing Speed

  • Children: 15–25 words per minute
  • Teenagers: 30–40 words per minute
  • Adults: 40–50 words per minute
  • Professional typists: 65–100+ words per minute

Many people can improve their typing speed with regular practice. Typing tests and typing exercises help develop muscle memory and increase both speed and accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Practice typing every day.
  • Focus on accuracy before speed.
  • Use typing exercises and practice paragraphs.
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard.
